True and false Gospel...

Posted on Feb, 2022 by Bro Vijay Kumar L
Gospel Doctrine


True and false Gospel ​​​​​Now in the world, virtues like education , health and human relationships have been commercialized and are now proficient avenues for engulfing money but what about the Gospel of christ .... the sad thing is , it has also been commercialised and is a thriving business run by people masquerading as evengelists and servants of God who have become millineors and are living in grand style , God only knows the fate of all these fancy preachers at the day of Judgement.

The problem with the false Gospel is , it doesn't
convict a man of his sin and enable him to know his true condition before God nor does it connect him to God by turning his heart from the interim world and make him one with God but only gives him some nice feeligs and make him live in a delusion, no wonder it began from the first century itself in the days of paul and peter, the propogaters of this convenient Gospel will only produce false believers , false revivals and false churches. The tragedy is, simple people are made to believe this fallacy and are led astray.

True Gospel makes a person honestly and radically repent of his sin like the tax collector at the temple who beat his chest and could not even Raise his head towards heaven and said "Lord forgive me a sinner" (Lk ) it will refashion the heart like zachaeus who looking onto Jesus wanted to return money back fourfold to whom he had cheated in his profession. True Gospel pricks the heart and leads them to repentance like the people who had gathered to hear Peter on the pentecost day, that is what a loyal servant of God and a true church must be doing

Bro. Lunjala. VIJAY KUMAR.
H.C.A. HUBLI.
