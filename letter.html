Letter for the Month

To, Dear Bro /Sis

Greetings,
I am awfully excited to write to you this letter, and its so nice to meet you in this way, we have entered into this new year while all around us, situation seems stark and ungentle, especially in the context of devastating pandemic, which has shaken the whole world. Our heartfelt sympathies to all who have lost their loved ones and we sincerely pray for all those who are on the recovery path.

Thank God for allowing us to step into this new year, but isnt is there a purpose for God to do so? taking some time to sit and figure out of how I am going to spend this year can work wonders in our life, instead of mindlessly allowing days to go by without a plan and purpose I have been a Christian from the past thirty years and I have come to know that the greatest thing on earth is to be a Christian, a Hollywood or Bollywood star, sports star, a business tycoon or a world leader is no match to a Christian, to think otherwise is just fooling ourselves.

I hope you have seen our first page of our website with three glorious statements made by our lord Jesus Christ that is “Come unto me (Mat 11:28), learn from me (Mat 11:29), and follow me (Mat 16:24) our Christian life must be based on these three principals where we keep on coming to Jesus and keep on learning from His glorious life of how he humbled himself and obeyed God till the end.

Thanks for reading this letter, I want to write letters like this once in a month to encourage you in love, you can also do that in return and encourage me as well.

“Your Friend in Christ”
Bro, Vijaykumar. L
